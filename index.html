Our Services

Land Disputes

Handling cases related to disputes over land ownership, boundaries, title verification, and encroachment.

Land Acquisition

Assisting with legal aspects of government or private land acquisition, compensation claims, and rehabilitation.

Property Documentation

Preparing and verifying documents like sale deeds, lease agreements, and gift deeds to ensure legality and compliance with revenue laws.

Revenue Records

Handling corrections and updates in land records, including mutations, entries, and rectification of errors.

Agricultural Land Issues

Addressing issues related to the sale, transfer, and inheritance of agricultural lands, especially for farmers and rural property owners.

Conversion of Land Use

Assisting clients in converting land use from agricultural to non-agricultural, including compliance with Gujarat's specific rules and procedures.

Tenancy and Tenure Laws

Handling matters related to tenancy rights, landlord-tenant disputes, and inheritance of tenancy rights.

Appeals and Representation

Representing clients before revenue authorities, including the Collector, Deputy Collector, and Gujarat Revenue Tribunal, in appeals and revisions.

Legal Consultation on Land Policies

Advising clients on various state land policies, amendments in revenue laws, and implications for property ownership and transactions.

Court Representation

Representation in civil courts, High Court, and the Supreme Court for various legal matters.
